Using our knowledge of mirrors and the Law of Reflection, we made a small interactive diagram for your enjoyment that illustrative soem of the concepts you should have read and understood the basic concepts in the Learn page. The red lines from the bottom up are the incident ray and the reflected ray, and the dashed line is the normal line. Have fun!



Directions: Click above or below the black line to increase or decrease the measure of the incident angle, respectively. In case additional clarification is required, a video has been provided to facilitate the use of the program!



Just in case the program cannot load, a link has been provided so that you may download the program to run it!
